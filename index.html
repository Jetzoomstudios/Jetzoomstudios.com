    • What is #JetzoomButterLanding?

    #JetzoomButterLanding is a term coined by Jetzoom Studios to describe the perfect, smooth landing of an aircraft. It's a combination of skill, precision, and timing that results in a landing so smooth, it's like butter!

    How to Achieve a Butter Landing

    1. Maintain the correct approach speed.
    2. Ensure proper alignment with the runway.
    3. Flare at the right moment.
    4. Gently reduce power and let the aircraft settle onto the runway.
